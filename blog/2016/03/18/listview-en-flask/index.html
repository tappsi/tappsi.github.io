About Tappsi

Tappsi is an e-hailing mobile app conceived for Latin American cities. Launched in Bogota in 2012, Tappsi has reached over one million users and great traction among the taxi drivers in just a few months. Currently available in Colombia, Peru and Ecuador. Tappsi is on track to establishing itself as the market leader in Bogota, and take our in-depth knowledge (of the the Latin American taxi driver and passenger) and know-how to other key Latin American cities which are in need of a solution like Tappsi.
